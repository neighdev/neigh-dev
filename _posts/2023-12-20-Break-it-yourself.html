Some people think that software never breaks by itself, but when you build an application it is never in isolation. It is dependent on the build environment, web protocols and even changes in language, perception and culture.

So this causes applications to go kaput when you stop or pause development.

All this to say that I have updated Trilium a few times without really checking if my export job still works.
